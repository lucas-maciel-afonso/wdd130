Audience

Who Are the Target Customers?
Our target customers include:

Adventure Seekers:

Age: 18-35 years old.
Financial Situation: Moderate to high disposable income, often young professionals or college students.
Interests: Outdoor activities, adrenaline-pumping sports, nature exploration, social media sharing of experiences.
Families:

Age: Parents aged 30-50 with children aged 7-18.
Financial Situation: Stable to high income, often dual-income households.
Interests: Safe and fun family activities, outdoor recreation, educational and bonding experiences.
Corporate Groups:

Age: Employees and managers aged 25-55.
Financial Situation: Funded by company budgets, looking for premium team-building activities.
Interests: Team cohesion, leadership development, unique and engaging corporate events.
What Do They Want?
Adventure Seekers: Thrilling, challenging, and memorable rafting experiences.
Families: Safe, enjoyable, and family-friendly outings that cater to various age groups.
Corporate Groups: Effective and engaging team-building activities that foster teamwork and communication.
What Do They Expect to Find or What Questions Will Be Answered by Accessing the Website?
Adventure Seekers:

Expected Information: Detailed descriptions of rafting routes, difficulty levels, trip durations, and safety protocols.
Questions Answered: "What are the most exciting routes?", "Is this suitable for my skill level?", "How can I book a trip?"
Families:

Expected Information: Family-friendly trip options, safety measures for children, age and weight requirements, pricing.
Questions Answered: "Is this trip safe for my children?", "What do we need to bring?", "How long is the trip?"
Corporate Groups:

Expected Information: Customized corporate packages, benefits of team-building activities, testimonials from other businesses.
Questions Answered: "What team-building activities do you offer?", "How can these trips benefit my team?", "How do I book a corporate event?"
